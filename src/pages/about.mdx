# About 

## Hack The Winter

Hack The Winter - The Second Wave (Angry Bird Edition) is a 24 hour national-level hackathon aimed at 
encouraging innovation, creativity, and problem-solving among students and professionals. 
The hackathon will focus on solving real-world challenges through technology in domains such as 
sustainability, education, and smart automation.

## GEHU Bhimtal

Nestled in the serene hills of Bhimtal, Graphic Era Hill University
(GEHU Bhimtal) is a hub of innovation, research, and technology driven learning.
With modern infrastructure and expert faculty,
GEHU nurtures creativity, collaboration, and leadership

## \<We Code\\\>

WeCode is the official coding club of Graphic Era Hill University, Bhimtal,
driven by a passion for innovation and technology. We organize
hackathons, coding challenges, and workshops to inspire students to think
creatively, solve real-world problems, and grow as future developers and
tech leaders.
